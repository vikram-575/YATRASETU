Common questions

How do I book a trip?

Just message us on WhatsApp. Tell us your dates, group size, budget, and what you want to see. We will reply within 2 hours.

Do you only cover UP?

Right now we specialise in the full UP circuit — Lucknow, Ayodhya, Varanasi, Prayagraj, Agra, Mathura. We are expanding to Uttarakhand, Nepal, and Rajasthan soon.

Can you help elderly or disabled travellers?

Absolutely. We have arranged wheelchair-accessible vehicles, assisted darshan access, and medical support contacts for many families. Just tell us your needs and we plan accordingly.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash. We take a small advance to confirm your booking and the balance before your trip starts.

What if something goes wrong during the trip?

Call or WhatsApp us immediately. We have local contacts everywhere. Hotel issues, transport problems, medical emergencies — we handle it. You will never be stuck alone.

Can you arrange for NRIs flying in from abroad?

Yes, many of our clients are NRIs. We can arrange airport pickup from Lucknow, Delhi, or Varanasi airports and manage everything from arrival.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes. Groups of 6+ get discounted per-person rates. Ask us for a group quote on WhatsApp.
